I study American literature, mostly from the long nineteenth century, focusing on Indigenous American literatures and environmental humanities. I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate in English at Yale University, where I will begin as a tenure-track Assistant Professor in 2024. I got my PhD at UVA, where I held the Jefferson Fellowship. My work is forthcoming in Early American Literature, J19: The Journal of Nineteenth-Century Americanists, Poe Studies, and Studies in American Indian Literatures.
