Direct Discourse Lab
In 2020 I began assembling a database of direct discourse in novels. Based on this database, in 2022 I launched the Direct Discourse Lab, a collaboration with several undergraduate students at UVA. The project's large-scale goal is developing scholarly research out of the database's contents, which divides direct-discourse quotes into conversations and marks them on several aspects (e.g. verb marker, adverb marker, speaker). Currently we are working on a paper about conversations in Jane Austen's novels.
